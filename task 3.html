The Brihadeeswarar Temple, also known as Peruvudaiyar Kovil, is a magnificent example of Tamil architecture located in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu. Built in the 11th century by Raja Raja Chola I, it is one of the largest temples in India and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
